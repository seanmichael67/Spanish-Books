# 52 Spanish Preschool Vocabulary Books - Annual Calendar

**Target Audience:** Preschool (ages 2-5)  
**Language:** Spanish  
**Format:** Interactive books like "Mis Frutas y Verduras"  
**Delivery:** One book per week, nightly builds  

---
