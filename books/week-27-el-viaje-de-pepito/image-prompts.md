## Notes for Illustration
- **Consistent style throughout all pages**
- **Bright, warm color palette (blues, yellows, reds, greens)**
- **Child-safe, friendly representations of vehicles**
- **Simple backgrounds that don't distract from main subject**
- **Pepito should look consistent when he appears**
- **Vehicles should be slightly anthropomorphized (friendly faces if appropriate)**
- **Clear, easy-to-identify vehicle shapes for toddlers**
- **Positive, joyful energy in every illustration**
