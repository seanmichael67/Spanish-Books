NOTES FOR ILLUSTRATOR:
- Keep facial expressions clear and exaggerated (preschoolers learning to read emotions)
- Use consistent character design across all pages (same child or similar style)
- Backgrounds should be simple but provide context for each emotion
- Colors should match the emotion (bright for happy/excited, softer for sad/tired, etc.)
- Ensure all illustrations are age-appropriate and comforting
- Diversity in representation is important
- Each image should tell a story that reinforces the Spanish text
