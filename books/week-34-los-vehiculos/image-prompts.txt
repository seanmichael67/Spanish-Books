DALL-E 3 Image Prompts for "Los Vehículos de Mi Ciudad"
Week 34 - Transportation Theme
Style: Warm, colorful, child-friendly illustrations with consistent cartoon style throughout

========================================
PAGE 1 - City Overview
========================================
A cheerful cartoon cityscape with various colorful vehicles. Bright sunny day with simple geometric buildings in pastel colors. In the foreground, show a red car, yellow school bus, and bicycle. Cute, friendly style perfect for 2-5 year olds. Vibrant colors with soft edges. No text.

========================================
PAGE 2 - El Carro (The Car)
========================================
A shiny red cartoon car with big friendly headlights that look like eyes, driving on a simple road. The car should be cute and approachable, with rounded edges and a cheerful expression. Blue sky background, green grass on sides. Child-friendly illustration style with bold colors.

========================================
PAGE 3 - El Autobús (The Bus)
========================================
A bright yellow school bus with happy children waving from the windows. The bus should have a friendly face (headlights as eyes, grille as smile). Simple cartoon style, cheerful and inviting. Background shows a simple road and blue sky with fluffy white clouds. Perfect for preschool age.

========================================
PAGE 4 - La Bicicleta (The Bicycle)
========================================
A colorful bicycle with training wheels, shown against a park background with trees and flowers. The bicycle should be kid-sized with a basket on front. Bright rainbow colors, sunny day. Style should be playful and encouraging for young children learning to ride. Simple, clean illustration.

========================================
PAGE 5 - El Camión de Bomberos (Fire Truck)
========================================
A big, bold red fire truck with ladder on top, friendly cartoon style. Show the truck from a 3/4 angle to display its size and details like hoses, lights, and wheels. Dalmatian dog mascot visible in window. Heroic but not scary - warm and approachable for young children. Bright colors.

========================================
PAGE 6 - El Avión (The Airplane)
========================================
A white and blue cartoon airplane flying through fluffy clouds in a bright blue sky. The plane should have a friendly face on the nose, with wings visible. Trail of white vapor behind. Sun peeking from corner. Peaceful, dreamy atmosphere. Child-friendly aviation illustration.

========================================
PAGE 7 - El Tren (The Train)
========================================
A colorful cartoon steam train with multiple cars, chugging along railroad tracks. Show steam puffing from smokestack in fluffy clouds. The train engine should have a happy face. Countryside background with green hills and flowers. Vintage-inspired but modern color palette. Whimsical and fun.

========================================
PAGE 8 - El Helicóptero (The Helicopter)
========================================
A friendly cartoon helicopter in sky blue and white, hovering in the air with rotor blades visible. The helicopter should have large windows showing a smiling pilot. Clouds in background, blue sky. Not military or serious - playful and gentle for preschoolers. Show motion with subtle blur on rotors.

========================================
PAGE 9 - El Barco (The Boat)
========================================
A cheerful sailboat with bright colored sails (red, yellow, blue) floating on calm blue water. Show gentle waves, seagulls in distance, sun reflecting on water. The boat should have a friendly, cartoonish appearance. Peaceful maritime scene perfect for young children. Warm, inviting colors.

========================================
PAGE 10 - Finale (All Vehicles)
========================================
A celebratory scene showing miniature versions of all the vehicles together: car, bus, bicycle, fire truck, airplane, train, helicopter, and boat. Arranged in a cheerful group composition as if taking a "family photo." Confetti or stars in background. Joyful, festive atmosphere celebrating transportation. Colorful and fun!

========================================
STYLE NOTES FOR ALL IMAGES:
- Consistent cartoon illustration style
- Warm, inviting color palette
- Soft edges, no sharp angles
- Age-appropriate (2-5 years)
- Friendly, non-threatening
- High contrast for visibility
- No text or words in images
- Focus on one main subject per image
- Simple, uncluttered backgrounds
