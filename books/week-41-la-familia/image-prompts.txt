DALL-E 3 Image Prompts for "La Familia de Sofía"
=======================================================

Art Style: Warm, colorful, child-friendly illustrations with a consistent soft watercolor style. Characters should have warm skin tones reflecting Hispanic heritage. Bright, cheerful colors throughout. Simple backgrounds with minimal detail to keep focus on family members.

---

PAGE 1: Cover - Sofia introduces herself
Prompt: A cheerful 4-year-old Hispanic girl with dark wavy hair and brown eyes, wearing a yellow dress with flowers, standing in front of a colorful family portrait on the wall. She's waving with a big smile. Warm watercolor style, soft edges, bright happy colors, simple background. Illustration for children's book.

---

CONSISTENCY NOTES:
- Keep the same facial features for Sofia throughout (dark wavy hair, brown eyes, yellow dress)
- All family members should have warm skin tones reflecting Hispanic heritage
- Use consistent warm color palette: yellows, oranges, pinks, light blues, greens
- Soft watercolor edges, not sharp digital lines
- Simple, uncluttered backgrounds
- Expressions should be warm and inviting
- Age-appropriate detail level for 2-5 year olds
