# La Lluvia y el Sol 🌧️☀️
**Week 24 - Weather Book**

## Overview
An interactive Spanish book teaching weather vocabulary through the natural cycle of rain and sunshine. Perfect for ages 2-5.

## Key Vocabulary (8 words)
1. **el cielo** - the sky
2. **las nubes** - the clouds
3. **la lluvia** - the rain
4. **el paraguas** - the umbrella
5. **los charcos** - the puddles
6. **el sol** - the sun
7. **el arcoíris** - the rainbow
8. **el día** - the day

## Learning Objectives
- Weather vocabulary in Spanish
- Understanding rain and sun cycle
- Basic nature observation
- Sequencing (before/after concept)

## Story Arc
The book follows a simple weather sequence:
1. Clear sky with clouds
2. Clouds turn gray
3. Rain begins to fall
4. Child uses umbrella
5. Puddles form on ground
6. Sun comes out
7. Rainbow appears
8. Beautiful sunny day

## Educational Value
- **Language:** 8 new Spanish words with pronunciation guides
- **Science:** Basic water cycle and weather patterns
- **Observation:** Teaches children to notice weather changes
- **Sequencing:** Understanding natural progression of events

## Parent/Teacher Tips
- Connect to real weather experiences
- Ask "¿Qué tiempo hace hoy?" (What's the weather today?)
- Go outside and observe together
- Make it playful: jump in puddles, feel rain, watch clouds

## Extension Activities
1. **Weather Chart:** Track daily weather for a week
2. **Rainbow Art:** Create rainbows with paint or crayons
3. **Umbrella Craft:** Make paper umbrellas
4. **Weather Song:** Sing about rain and sun in Spanish
