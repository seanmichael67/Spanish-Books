## Technical Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio:** Landscape (16:10 or 4:3)
- **Style Consistency:** All images should feel like they're from the same book
- **Color Palette:** Bright but not oversaturated, warm and inviting
- **Target Audience:** Ages 2-5 years
- **Mood:** Friendly, educational, joyful, safe

## Notes for Generation
- Avoid scary or overly realistic animal features
- Keep backgrounds simple but detailed enough to be engaging
- Each animal should have a distinct personality through facial expression
- Maintain consistent lighting and color temperature across all images
- Animals should look approachable and huggable, never threatening
