# DALL-E 3 Image Prompts - La Orquesta de los Animales

## Style Guidelines
- Warm, colorful, child-friendly illustrations
- Consistent cartoon art style throughout
- Soft edges, friendly expressions
- Bright, engaging colors
- Simple backgrounds to keep focus on characters
- Age-appropriate for 2-5 year olds

---

## Page 1: Cover
**Prompt:**
A cheerful cartoon illustration of a diverse group of animal musicians on a stage with musical notes floating around them. Include a cat, dog, rabbit, frog, bird, and elephant, all holding different instruments. Warm, vibrant colors. Playful, welcoming style. Stage curtains in the background. Musical notes and sparkles in the air. Children's book illustration style, soft edges, friendly faces.

## Notes for Image Generation
- Keep the art style consistent across all images
- Each animal should have the same appearance throughout the book
- Musical instruments should be recognizable and accurate
- Background colors should coordinate with the book's purple gradient theme
- Facial expressions should be joyful and engaging for young children
- Ensure all images are suitable for ages 2-5 (nothing scary or overly complex)
