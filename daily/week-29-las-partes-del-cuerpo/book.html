🌟 Mi Cuerpo Maravilloso 🌟

[Illustration: A happy child standing with arms spread wide, showing their whole body with a big smile]

¡Hola! Soy un niño especial.
¡Vamos a descubrir mi cuerpo!

(OH-lah! Soy oon NEE-nyo es-peh-see-AHL. VAH-mos ah des-koo-BREER mee KWER-poh!)

Hello! I am a special child.
Let's discover my body!

👀 Los Ojos

[Illustration: Close-up of a child's smiling face with big, sparkling eyes looking at colorful butterflies]

Con mis ojos, yo puedo ver.
¡Veo mariposas de muchos colores!

(Kohn mees OH-hos, yoh PWEH-doh behr. BEH-oh mah-ree-POH-sahs deh MOO-chos koh-LOH-res!)

With my eyes, I can see.
I see butterflies of many colors!

Nueva Palabra:

Los ojos (lohs OH-hos) = eyes

Ver (behr) = to see

👂 Las Orejas

[Illustration: Child with hand cupped to ear, listening to birds singing in a tree with musical notes floating]

Con mis orejas, yo puedo oír.
¡Oigo los pájaros cantar!

(Kohn mees oh-REH-hahs, yoh PWEH-doh oh-EER. OY-goh lohs PAH-hah-rohs kahn-TAHR!)

With my ears, I can hear.
I hear the birds singing!

Nueva Palabra:

Las orejas (lahs oh-REH-hahs) = ears

Oír (oh-EER) = to hear

👃 La Nariz

[Illustration: Child smelling a bouquet of colorful flowers with eyes closed and happy expression]

Con mi nariz, yo puedo oler.
¡Huelo las flores bonitas!

(Kohn mee nah-REES, yoh PWEH-doh oh-LEHR. WEH-loh lahs FLOH-res boh-NEE-tahs!)

With my nose, I can smell.
I smell the pretty flowers!

Nueva Palabra:

La nariz (lah nah-REES) = nose

Oler (oh-LEHR) = to smell

👄 La Boca

[Illustration: Child eating a juicy slice of watermelon with juice dripping, big smile showing enjoyment]

Con mi boca, yo puedo comer.
¡Como sandía dulce y rica!

(Kohn mee BOH-kah, yoh PWEH-doh koh-MEHR. KOH-moh sahn-DEE-ah DOOL-seh ee REE-kah!)

With my mouth, I can eat.
I eat sweet and delicious watermelon!

Nueva Palabra:

La boca (lah BOH-kah) = mouth

Comer (koh-MEHR) = to eat

👋 Las Manos

[Illustration: Child's hands playing with colorful building blocks, creating a tower]

Con mis manos, yo puedo tocar.
¡Toco y juego con mis bloques!

(Kohn mees MAH-nohs, yoh PWEH-doh toh-KAHR. TOH-koh ee HWEH-goh kohn mees BLOH-kes!)

With my hands, I can touch.
I touch and play with my blocks!

Nueva Palabra:

Las manos (lahs MAH-nohs) = hands

Tocar (toh-KAHR) = to touch

🦶 Los Pies

[Illustration: Child running and jumping joyfully in a park with autumn leaves flying around]

Con mis pies, yo puedo correr.
¡Corro, salto y bailo feliz!

(Kohn mees pee-EHS, yoh PWEH-doh koh-REHR. KOH-rroh, SAHL-toh ee BYE-loh feh-LEES!)

With my feet, I can run.
I run, jump, and dance happily!

Nueva Palabra:

Los pies (lohs pee-EHS) = feet

Correr (koh-REHR) = to run

❤️ El Corazón

[Illustration: Child hugging parent with hearts floating around them, showing love and warmth]

Con mi corazón, yo puedo amar.
¡Amo a mi familia!

(Kohn mee koh-rah-SOHN, yoh PWEH-doh ah-MAHR. AH-moh ah mee fah-MEE-lee-ah!)

With my heart, I can love.
I love my family!

Nueva Palabra:

El corazón (ehl koh-rah-SOHN) = heart

Amar (ah-MAHR) = to love

✨ Todo Mi Cuerpo ✨

[Illustration: Child dancing in a circle with body parts labeled, surrounded by stars and hearts, looking confident and happy]

¡Mi cuerpo es maravilloso!
Puedo ver, oír, oler, comer,
tocar, correr y amar.
¡Soy especial tal como soy!

(Mee KWER-poh es mah-rah-bee-YOH-soh! PWEH-doh behr, oh-EER, oh-LEHR, koh-MEHR, toh-KAHR, koh-REHR ee ah-MAHR. Soy es-peh-see-AHL tahl KOH-moh soy!)

My body is wonderful!
I can see, hear, smell, eat,
touch, run, and love.
I am special just as I am!

Repaso de Palabras:

Los ojos • Las orejas • La nariz

La boca • Las manos • Los pies

El corazón • El cuerpo
