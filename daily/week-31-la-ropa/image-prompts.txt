DALL-E 3 Image Prompts for "La Ropa de Sofía"
Week 31 - Clothing Vocabulary Book
===========================================

STYLE NOTES:
- Warm, friendly children's book illustration style
- Bright, cheerful colors
- Consistent character design (Sofia: young Hispanic girl, brown hair, friendly smile)
- Age-appropriate and culturally authentic
- Clean, simple backgrounds that don't distract from the main subject

GENERATION NOTES:
- Generate all 9 images with consistent character design
- Sofia should look like the same girl in each image
- Maintain consistent bedroom environment across indoor scenes
- Use warm, inviting color palette throughout
- Focus on expressions that show independence, joy, and confidence
- Keep backgrounds simple but supportive of the story

CLOTHING ITEMS TO FEATURE:
- Pink socks (calcetines rosados)
- Blue jeans (pantalones azules)
- Yellow t-shirt with sun (camiseta amarilla)
- Red sneakers (zapatos rojos)
- Purple sweater (suéter morado)
- Colorful hat (gorro)
- Matching scarf (bufanda)
