DALL-E 3 Image Prompts for "Los Números - Contando en el Parque"
Week 32 - Numbers
===================================================================

Style Guide:
- Warm, vibrant colors
- Child-friendly, playful illustration style
- Consistent art style throughout (watercolor/digital painting)
- Bright and cheerful atmosphere
- Age-appropriate for 2-5 year olds
- Large, clear focal objects for counting
- No text in images

===================================================================

Page 1 - Title Page:
"A beautiful sunny park scene with a colorful playground, green grass, blue sky, and happy children playing. Warm watercolor illustration style, vibrant colors, cheerful atmosphere, perfect day. View from park entrance with winding path leading into the park. Preschool children illustration, friendly and inviting."

---

Page 2 - Uno (One Sun):
"A large, smiling sun in a bright blue sky above a park. The sun has a friendly face with warm rays extending outward. Watercolor illustration style, one single sun as the focal point, vibrant yellow and orange colors, cheerful and warm feeling. Simple composition for preschool counting book."

---

Page 3 - Dos (Two Ducks):
"Two adorable ducks swimming together in a peaceful pond. The ducks are side by side, with ripples in the water around them. Lily pads and reeds in background. Watercolor illustration style, warm colors, exactly two ducks clearly visible for counting. Preschool-friendly, cute and engaging."

---

Page 4 - Tres (Three Trees):
"Three tall trees with lush green leaves standing in a park. The trees are clearly distinct and easy to count. Grass at the base, blue sky in background. Watercolor illustration style, vibrant greens and browns, clear composition showing exactly three trees. Child-friendly park scene."

Page 5 - Cuatro (Four Flowers):
"Four beautiful flowers of different colors in a garden bed. One red flower, one yellow flower, one pink flower, and one purple flower. Each flower is distinct and easy to count. Watercolor illustration style, vibrant colors, cheerful garden scene. Preschool counting book illustration."

Page 6 - Cinco (Five Butterflies):
"Five colorful butterflies flying among flowers in a sunny garden. Each butterfly has distinct colors and patterns - blue, orange, yellow, pink, and purple. Butterflies are clearly visible and easy to count. Watercolor illustration style, magical and whimsical, bright colors. Child-friendly nature scene."

Page 7 - Seis (Six Birds):

Page 8 - Siete (Seven Balloons):

Page 9 - Ocho (Eight Stars):
"Eight twinkling stars in a dark blue night sky. The stars are bright and sparkling with different sizes but all clearly visible for counting. Deep blue and purple night sky with a peaceful atmosphere. Watercolor illustration style, magical nighttime scene. Each star clearly distinct for preschool counting."

Page 10 - Celebration:
"A happy preschool child with arms raised in celebration, surrounded by confetti and sparkles. Big smile, colorful background with numbers 1-8 subtly floating around. Watercolor illustration style, joyful and proud moment, warm and encouraging atmosphere. Achievement celebration for preschoolers."

===================================================================

Notes:
- All images should maintain consistent watercolor/digital painting style
- Objects should be large, clear, and easy for young children to count
- Each image focuses on the specific number being taught
- Colors should be vibrant and engaging
- No text or numbers should appear in the images themselves
- Background elements should not distract from the countable objects
