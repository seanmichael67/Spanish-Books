TECHNICAL SPECS:
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (landscape) or 1:1 (square) preferred

- Target Audience: Ages 2-5 years
- Cultural Sensitivity: Diverse representation, accurate animal depictions

CONSISTENCY NOTES:
- Maintain similar lighting (warm, bright)
- Keep character style consistent (rounded, soft edges)
- Use similar color saturation across all images
- Backgrounds should complement but not overwhelm subjects
- All animals should look friendly and approachable
