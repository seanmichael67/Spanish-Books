
# Idea: "La Biblioteca Magica" (The Magical Library)
# Theme: Exploring a library, finding a book, learning to read/discover.
# Target: Ages 2-5
# Language: Spanish

---

Page 1: Cover
Text: La Biblioteca Magica
Image Prompt: A colorful, inviting children's library with soft rugs, many shelves of books, and a friendly, whimsical atmosphere. Pixar-style, warm lighting, bright colors.

Page 2:
Text: Mira, una gran biblioteca. Hay muchos libros.
Image Prompt: A cute toddler standing in front of a giant bookshelf filled with colorful books, looking excited.

Page 3:
Text: Vamos a buscar un libro especial.
Image Prompt: The toddler walking toward a shelf, pointing at a shiny, glowing blue book.

Page 4:
Text: ¡Lo encontré! Es de color azul.
Image Prompt: Close up of the toddler's small hands grabbing the glowing blue book from the shelf.

Page 5:
Text: Abrimos el libro. ¿Qué hay adentro?
Image Prompt: The toddler sitting on a cozy, oversized beanbag chair opening the book wide.

Page 6:
Text: ¡Mira! Un pequeño conejo salta.
Image Prompt: Inside the book's illustrations, a cute, playful white rabbit is jumping out from the pages.

Page 7:
Text: El conejo busca zanahorias ricas.
Image Prompt: The rabbit sitting in a garden patch nibbling on a carrot. Whimsical cartoon style.

Page 8:
Text: Nos gusta leer historias bonitas.
Image Prompt: The toddler happily looking at the rabbit inside the book, smiling big.

Page 9:
Text: Leer es muy divertido. ¡Adiós libro!
Image Prompt: The toddler closing the book, hugging it gently. The library room is quiet and happy.

Page 10:
Text: Fin.
Image Prompt: A peaceful scene of the library at sunset, with a few books glowing softly.
